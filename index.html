Welcome to CONSTRUCTOPIA!

A strategic game of chance and building skills.

Quick Overview:

  • 2 Players
  • 4 Dice (2 per player)
  • 26 Building Cards (2 levels)
  • Multiple Block Shapes
  • Special Wildcard Mechanics

Game Components

How to Play

Game Flow:

  • Both players roll their two dice simultaneously
  • Each player sums up their dice values
  • Players select cards based on their dice sums
  • If both players get the same sum, they must use the wildcard
  • Players attempt to build the structure shown on their card

Building Challenges

The Tower Challenge

Build the tallest stable structure using only cubic blocks. Time limit: 5 minutes

Bridge Builder

Create a bridge between two points using arches and prisms. Must support weight of one cube.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if pieces fall during building?

Players must restart their structure if pieces fall during construction.
